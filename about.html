January 3

Who or what are your inspirations?
Eminem, Lil Wayne, J Cole, Tupac
When did you start writing raps?
Around three years ago, at the start of high school (2012) (began writing a little bit in eighth grade)
What is your typical production process?
Make the beat (using fruityloops), sometimes sample old school stuff Start developing the chorus, then build the verses around the chorus Usually songs revolve around personal experiences, emotions towards specific events, etc.
What program do you use?
Fruity Loops (fl studios) and audacity
Personal favorite songs:
“Swear, Vent, and Loved You, though I feel like King Filthy had the best wordplay”
Why is your name “filthynate”?
“I decided to take the name after a business my father runs.”
